Cookies

Sans Rival

a classic and an all-time favorite dessert of Filipinos. It is a frozen cookie sandwiches, coated in cashew crumbs, and made with two cashew-meringue wafers held together with a thick layer of buttercream.

Silvanas

a cookie version of Sans Rival made from similar ingredients. It is a frozen cookie consisting of a layer of buttercream sandwiched between two cashew-meringue wafers coated with cookie crumbs.

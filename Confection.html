Confection

Pastillias

Pastillas also known as Pastillas de Leche or Pastiyema is a milk-based confectionery with origins in the town of San Miguel in Bulacan, Philippines. It is made from either carabao or cow milk or both.

Yema

Yema, a sweet custard confectionery in the Philippines. It is made from egg yolks, milk, and sugar. It is known for the word “egg yolk” in Spanish.

Drinks

Taho

Taho is a Filipino dessert that gives sweetness in our mouths. It is made with silken tofu, brown sugar syrup, and sago pearls. It is mostly served as warm and can also be enjoyed chilled.

Sago't Gulaman

Gulaman is a popular drink among Filipinos by its chilling sensation. It is made from a bar of or powdered form of dried agar or carrageenan used to make jelly-like desserts. It is served as iced or cooled.

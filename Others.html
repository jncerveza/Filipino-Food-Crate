Ube Halaya

Ube halaya is a famous Filipino dessert made from purple yam. It consists of mashed purple yams in plain milk, coconut milk, or condensed milk alongside sugar and vanilla essence. It has a sticky texture and a unique purple color.

Maja Blanca

Maja blanca, a thick gelatine dessert with a delicate flavour and a creamy white color. It is made with coconut milk, sweet corn kernels, and toasted coconut. It is mostly served on Christmas holydays.

Minatamis na Saging

Minatamis na saging, a dessert dish sweetened by cooking in a sweet mixture. It is made from water, brown sugar, salt, and vanilla extract.

Halo-Halo

Halo-halo, the ultimate summer time snack of Filipinos. It is a layered dessert consisting of sweetened beans, fruits, shaved ice drizzled with evaporated milk, and ice cream. It is sold all around the Philippine establishments such as restaurants and fast foods.

Ice Candy

Ice candy is the most favourite Filipino treats in summer because of its refreshing sensation. They are frozen juice in a plastic like pop ice. There are different flavors such as orange, mango, buko, avocado, melon, grape, and chocolate.

Ice Scramble

Also known as “Skrambol”. Filipinos always find their way to taste its sensational flavors. It is a Filipino frozen dessert made from shaved ice with banana extract and evaporated milk with sugar. It can also be added by toppings such as powdered milk, marshmallows, strawberry syrup, chocolate syrup, pinipig, tapioca pearls, and sprinkles.

Fruit Salad

Feel loved and feel healthy by eating fruit salad, a perfect family treat. It is a dish with strawberries, pineapple, honeydew, watermelon, grapes, and kiwifruit. Other recipes may call for the addition of nuts, fruit juices, certain vegetables, yogurt, or other ingredients.

‘Dirty’ Ice Cream

Also known as “Sorbetes”. It is a traditional ice cream from the Philippines and sold by ‘naglalako’ or a push stand. Made by the use of coconut milk or/and carabao milk. It can be paired with cones, breads, or even wafers.

Mais Con Yelo

Mais Con Yelo, a delicious and refreshing summer treat. It weet contains corn, sweetened shaved ice, and corn flake cereal topped with homemade corn ice cream. It is perfect for a summer holy day.

Buko Pandan

Buko Pandan is a popular Filipino Dessert it gives a delicious and soft feelings inside our mouths. It consists of young coconut, pandan leaves (or Screwpine leaves), and sago pearls. It is visually similar to Buko Salad.

Mango Graham

Mango Graham also called Mango Float, its coldness and sweetness that makes it a popular dessert in the Philippines. It contains layered graham crackers, sweetened cream, mangoes and enjoy.

Fried Goods

Buchi

Buchi is famously served in the Chowking of the Philippines a establishment that sells Chinese like foods. The most common type of filling is the sweetened red beans paste, also ube and milk mixtures. Fillings can be changed depending on your taste.

Chicharon

Chicharon is a famous and loved snack by Filipinos by its crunchiness and flavorous feeling. It is pork skin cracklings that are made out of pork rind. They are boiled and seasoned hen sun-dried and deep-fried.

Turon

Turon is a very popular Filipino snack because of its sweetness and crunchiness. It is made from ripe saba banana, jackfruit, and brown sugar are rolled together in a flour lumpia wrapper. To create its crunchiness, it is then deep fried to a golden crisp.

Banana Cue

Banana cue is a popular street food in the Philippines and it gives banana flavoured sweetness. It is made with saba bananas coated with sugar. To make the sugar coat the banana it is deep fried and skewered on a stick.

Maruya

Maruya is a famous fanned shape street food in the streets of the Philippines. It is made from saba bananas and egg mixed flour. For its masterpiece appearance it is then deep fried and sprinkled with sugar.

Kwek Kwek

Kwek-kwek is a popular street food in the Philippines with an egg coated with orange batter. It is made by hard-boiled eggs in orange batter. It is deep fried and skewered.

Empanada

Empanada is famous around the world, especially in Latin America, Spain, and Portugal. They are made by folded dough over the filling and sealing it before cooking.

Dynamite

Dynamite is a famous snack in the Philippines it is gives the hottest sensation of a snack that could ever be. It is made by silling haba (long green chilli peppers) wrapped with egg crepe, stuffed giniling (ground beef or pork), cheese, tocino, ham, bacon, tuna, and shredded chicken. After rolling the crepe it is then deep fried to a golden crisp.

Carioca

Carioca is a chewy, sticky, and sweet snack or dessert here in the Philipines. A fried food made of sweet rice flour and has caramelized sugar for coating.

Adobong Baboy

Filipino Adobong Baboy is a favorite dish of Filipinos, and due to its high popularity, many consider it to be the national dish of the Philippines. Adobo dishes can be made with pork, chicken, beef, or a combination of the two.

Bicol Express

Bicol Express is a popular Filipino dish with coconut milk, shrimp paste and slices of pork cooked with chili peppers. The perfect meal for a spicy lover.

Bopis

Bopis is a spicy Filipino delicacy made from minced pork lungs and heart cooked with onions, garlic and chili peppers. It is popular as a starter and is often used as a main course.

Crispy Pata

Crispy Pata is a snack for pork lovers. It has crispy pork skin filled with spicy and tender meat. Crispy Pata is commonly defined as fried pork ribs or shanks, but are actually cut from the hock to the feet.

Dinuguan

Dinuguan, also known as chocolate meat or blood pudding, is a hearty dish made from cubic pork, pork blood and spices. This classic Filipino pork stew is generous and tasty, and is delicious as a main course of rice or as a mid day snack with puto.

History

“Mais Con Yelo” or “Mais con Hielo” for Spanish is a corn (Mais in tagalog) dessert that is topped on crushed ice. The corn is prepared boiled and usually sweet and savory. This dessert is also known around the world. Just like halo-halo you can add other ingredients on this dessert, but it usually consists of sweet ingredients like “saging saba”. Corns also have different varieties and colors, so if you're artistic enough you may want to use different kernels from different corns.

Make One

Ingredients

This is how you usually prepare a “mais con yelo”.(Courtesy to Pilipinas Recipes or pilipinasrecipes.com
  • Sweet Corn (canned or Frozen)
  • Shaved Ice
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • Evaporated Milk
  • White Sugar
  • Keso (Optional)
  • Saging na Saba (Optional)
  • Corn Flakes (Optional)

Procedure

  • Layer the Sweet Corn and the Shaved Ice in a Glass
  • Add Milk and Sugar, Condensed Milk, Evaporated Milk (adjust according to taste) and stir with a spoon.
  • Add other ingredients like Keso, Saging na Saba or CornFlakes.
  • Serve immediately.

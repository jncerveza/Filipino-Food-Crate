Ice Scramble

History

Just like Mais Con Yelo, Ice Scramble or Iskramble in tagalog. Is an ice shaved dessert with an extract of evaporated milk and banana extract. Add toppings like powdered milk, marshmallows, strawberry syrup, chocolate syrup, pinipig, tapioca pearls, and sprinkles and it's now a super sweet and delicate dessert. Iskramble is usually dyed by the vendors, most of the time it's pink or violet. This dessert is popular for youngsters, because of its bright colors and sweet toppings. The dessert is called scrambled because before eating it you should “scramble” the toppings and the shaved mixture first.


https://www.panlasangpinoyrecipes.com/ice-scramble-iskrambol-recipe/

Ingredients

  • 3-4 cups shaved ice
  • 1-2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 cup Evaporated milk
  • 1/2 tsp banana extract/flavoring
  • 6-8 drops of red food color for pinkish color
  • Optional Toppings: powdered milk chocolate syrup, strawberry syrup, mini marshmallows, candy sprinkles, rice crispies

Procedure

  1. Mix evaporated milk, banana extract, red food color and sugar in a bowl.

  3. Place in serving glasses or cups..

  4. Top it with optional toppings.

  5. Enjoy!

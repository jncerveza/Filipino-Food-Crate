Pandesal

Pandesal is one of the most famous bread in the Philippines because of its softness. It is made by rolling the dough into long logs straight to fine bread crumbs. It can be paired with hot chocolates or coffees of your choice.

Buko Pie

Buko pie is a traditional pie in the Philippines made with coconut. It has a flaky pie crust and a smooth and sweet filling when you eat it.

Puto Bumbong

Puto Bumbong is a famous dish that Filipino likes and it is mostly sold on the upcoming christmas. It is type of Filipino purple rice cake which is prepared by steaming ground purple rice mixture inside a bamboo tube. It is topped with margarine (or butter), freshly grated coconut, and muscovado sugar.

Chicharon

Chicharon is a famous and loved snack by Filipinos by its crunchiness and flavorous feeling. It is pork skin cracklings that are made out of pork rind. They are boiled and seasoned hen sun-dried and deep-fried.

Turon

Turon is a very popular Filipino snack because of its sweetness and crunchiness. It is made from ripe saba banana, jackfruit, and brown sugar are rolled together in a flour lumpia wrapper. To create its crunchiness, it is then deep fried to a golden crisp.

Kwek-kwek

Kwek-kwek is a popular street food in the Philippines with an egg coated with orange batter. It is made by hard-boiled eggs in orange batter. It is deep fried and skewered.

Taho

Taho is a Filipino dessert that gives sweetness in our mouths. It is made with silken tofu, brown sugar syrup, and sago pearls. It is mostly served as warm and can also be enjoyed chilled.

Binatog

Binatog a popular Filipino street food and snack that gives a different kind of taste and senstaion. It is made by soaking the mature white corn in water and salt until puffed, the soaked corns are then boiled until the skin is peeled off. It is topped with boiled corn kernels, grated coconut, salt and sugar.

Filipino Style Spaghetti

Filipino-style spaghetti is a snack and served on any special occasion. It has a sweet sauce which is the favorite of the Filipinos and makes it unique.

Ginataang Bilo bilo

Ginataang bilo bilo is one of the famous and favorite snacks or desserts of the Filipinos because of its sweetness and creaminess. Ginataang bilo bilo is cooked in sweet coconut milk together with mixed fruits.

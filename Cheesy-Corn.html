Cheesy Corn

History

Cheesy corn is a famous and popular snack and street food in the Philippines. Cheesy corn is made from corn kernels, cheese powder, butter, and sometimes it has sugar to make it sweet.


http://www.flyacecorp.com/recipes/details/cheesy-corn-snack

Ingredients

  • 1 can Jolly Whole Kernel Corn, 425g
  • 50 g cheese powder
  • 100 g margarine or butter

Procedure

  1. Drain Jolly Whole Corn and place in a bowl.

  2. Add some cheese powder and melted margarine or butter.

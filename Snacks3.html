Ensaymada

Every bakery in the Philippines sells ensaymada because it is so delicious. It has very rich dough with layers of butter and cheese.

Carioca

Carioca is a chewy, sticky, and sweet snack or dessert here in the Philipines. A fried food made of sweet rice flour and has caramelized sugar for coating.

Buchi

Buchi is famously served in the Chowking of the Philippines a establishment that sells Chinese like foods. The most common type of filling is the sweetened red beans paste, also ube and milk mixtures. Fillings can be changed depending on your taste.

Spanish Bread

Spanish bread is the Favorite ‘mirienda’ or afternoon snacks of Filipinos because of its multi layered flavor. It is rolled into a log enclosing within a sugary and buttery filling, and then rolled into breadcrumbs before baking. Perfect for afternoon snack.

Egg Pie

Egg pie is a sweet and creamy egg pie that can be eaten as a snack or dessert and is usually made with flour, milk, egg, and sugar.

Hopia

Another favourite afternoon snack of Filipinos is Hopia fillied with soft beans inside. It is made of thin flaky pastry, traditionally filled with mung bean paste (Munggo). There are more variation of flavor such as Ube or purple yam and the preferred flavour of Filipinos.

Dynamite

Dynamite is a famous snack in the Philippines it is gives the hottest sensation of a snack that could ever be. It is made by silling haba (long green chilli peppers) wrapped with egg crepe, stuffed giniling (ground beef or pork), cheese, tocino, ham, bacon, tuna, and shredded chicken. After rolling the crepe it is then deep fried to a golden crisp.

Lengua De Gato

Lengua De Gato is a long, thin, buttery, and crunchy Filipino cookie snack here in the Philippines. A famous snack made of flour, butter, sugar, and eggs only.

Inihaw na Isaw

Isaw is one of the street food ihaw-ihaw and a favorite snack of the Filipinos. Ihaw-ihaw is grilled street food and on a skewer.

Cheesy Corn

Cheesy corn is street food and a favorite snack of everyone. It consists of kernel corn, powder cheese, and butter only.

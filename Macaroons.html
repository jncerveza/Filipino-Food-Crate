Macaroons

History

Macaroon originated from Italy, it's a generic word to define any light and baked confection usually presented as small cakes or meringue-like cookies. But us Filipinos created our own version of it, we call it Coconut Macaroons. As the name suggests coconut macaroons are macaroons made from coconut, it's a baked dessert that is cupcake shaped.


https://panlasangpinoy.com/coconut-macaroons/

Ingredients

  • 14 ounces sweet shredded coconut or desiccated coconut
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 3 pieces raw eggs
  • 14 ounces condensed milk

Procedure

  5. In a mold (with paper cups if possible), place 1 tablespoon of the mixture on each of the cups

Chicken Dishes

Chicken Afritada

Chicken Afritada Recipe is another favorite dish in every Filipino home. Chicken is cooked with tomato sauce, bell peppers, potatoes, and carrots. You might have tried this at Fiesta, birthdays, and other popular Filipino celebrations.

Chicken Asado

Chicken Asado (also called Asadong Manok) is a spicy Filipino chicken stew cooked with potato and tomato sauce. A wonderful combination of spicy, sweet, sour and savory.

Chicken Curry

This chicken curry is cooked in Filipino style. Chicken dish with ginger and curry spices. The sauce is rich, creamy and flavorful. It goes well with warm white rice.

Chicken Igado

Igado is a dish that uses pork tenderloin and pig innards, which are popular in the Ilocos region. Cooked with a mixture of vinegar and soy sauce, it brings out a unique flavor. It has vegetables such as peas and red peppers that give the dish a beautiful and vibrant color.

Chicken Pastel

Chicken Pastel is a hearty stew of diced chicken, sausages, mushrooms, peas and carrots stewed in a rich creamy sauce. Based on Spanish pastel de pollo, the chicken filling is wrapped in flaky puff pastry and baked until golden.

Inasal na Manok

Inasal na Manok is a unique Filipino grilled chicken dish that originated in Bacolod and has become a staple of the entire Visayas region. We use a variety of chicken marinated in a mixture of vinegar and many spices such as lemongrass, garlic and ginger.

Pininyahang Manok

Popularly anglicized as pineapple chicken, Pininyahang Manok is a Filipino dish consisting of chicken stewed in pineapple, carrot, potato, pepper-based milk or coconut milk-based sauce. Some variations of the dish use chicken broth instead of milk.

Tinolang Manok

Tinolang Manok is a light and refreshing chicken soup. Using chicken, spinach and green papaya, this clean, clear soup is a popular home-cooked dish in the Philippines. It is great for cool days.

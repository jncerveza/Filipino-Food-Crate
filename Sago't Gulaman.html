Sago't Gulaman

History

Sago’t Gulaman is a drink or can be a snack at the same time, and a popular and favorite of the Filipinos, especially in a hot season. Sago’t gulaman is a local drink here in the Philippines that gives refreshment whenever you drink it and eat those pearls in a hot season.


https://www.pinoyrecipe.net/buko-pandan-sagot-gulaman-recipe/

Ingredients

    SALAD:
  • 2 pcs. fresh coconuts cut in half and scrape (yield 3¼ cups), reserved ½ cup
  • 2½ cups sago cooked
  • 3 cups pandan gulaman cooked, reseved ½ cup
  • 1 370ml alaska crema-asada, chilled
  • 1 370ml evaporated milk
  • 1 300ml condensed milk
  • ½ cup coconut water or water


    • TOPPINGS:
  • ½ cup pandan gulaman
  • ½ cup fresh coconut
  • Whipped cream optional


    • YOU ALSO NEED:
  • 12 pcs. 12oz. plastic cups

Procedure

    LET'S PREPARE OUR CONDIMENTS:

  1. First, you need to cook your sago, we need a half cup of big raw sago and cook in 1 liter of boiling water for 20 minutes.

  2. Drain, rinse several times, soak in water and keep chill for at least 8 hrs.

  3. Reboil for 15 minutes, and leave sago covered for another 30 minutes.

  4. Rinse and soak in water, and it’s ready to use.



    5. FOR THE GULAMAN:

  5. We’re going to cook a firm gulaman but if you prefer a softer gulaman adjust and add more water needed.

  6. But for this recipe, we need 1(25g) sachet of pandan flavored gulaman, 3½ cups of water and 1/3 cup of sugar.

  7. Combine in pot and stir until gulaman powder and sugar dissolved. Bring to a boil, while stirring continuously and pour it in any container and let it cool down.

  8. Chill and cut into cubes and set aside.



    9. LET'S PREPARE OUR COCONUT:

  9. Scrape coconut meat from the shell using a coconut scraper, try to avoid the brown bottom part as possible as you can.

  10. If fresh coconuts are not available, you can use a frozen coconut, but you have to thaw it first before using.



    11. LET'S MAKE OUR BUKO PANDAN WITH SAGO:

  11. In a large bowl, pour in chilled Alaska Crema-Asada, evaporated and sweetened condensed milk and stir until well blended.

  12. Add in, cooked gulaman, sago and coconut meat strings and mix well.

  13. Chill for at least 2 hours before serving.



    14. LET'S PREPARE OUR WHIPPED CREAM:

  14. In a mixing bowl, combine ½ cup frosty whip cream powder, ½ tsp pandan extract and ½ cup cold water.

  15. Let the powder soak for a sec. and whip on medium until frothy, then whip on high until double in volume and soft peaks form.



    16. ASSEMBLE BUKO PANDAN WITH SAGO:

  16. In a tall glass or plastic cups, fill cups at least 2/3’s full of buko pandan salad, add pandan whipped cream on top and fresh coconuts.

  17. Serve in a cup and enjoy it with your family.

Sans Rival

a classic and an all-time favorite dessert of Filipinos. It is a frozen cookie sandwiches, coated in cashew crumbs, and made with two cashew-meringue wafers held together with a thick layer of buttercream.

Silvanas

a cookie version of Sans Rival made from similar ingredients. It is a frozen cookie consisting of a layer of buttercream sandwiched between two cashew-meringue wafers coated with cookie crumbs.

Ube Halaya

Ube halaya is a famous Filipino dessert made from purple yam. It consists of mashed purple yams in plain milk, coconut milk, or condensed milk alongside sugar and vanilla essence. It has a sticky texture and a unique purple color.

Yema

Yema, a sweet custard confectionery in the Philippines. It is made from egg yolks, milk, and sugar. It is known for the word “egg yolk” in Spanish.

Polvoron

Polvoron, a Filipino shortbread cookie. Made from toasted flour, powdered milk, butter, and sugar. These crunchy and crumbly cookies are sweet and addicting.

Leche-flan

Leche-flan, a dessert that resembles crème caramel and caramel custard. It is made from eggs and milk with a soft caramel on top.

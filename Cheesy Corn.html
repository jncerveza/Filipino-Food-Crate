Cheesy Corn

History

Cheesy corn is a famous and popular snack and street food in the Philippines. Cheesy corn is made from corn kernels, cheese powder, butter, and sometimes it has sugar to make it sweet. It is usually sold outside the schools or sometimes peddled by the vendor from house to house.

Ingredients

  • 1 can Jolly Whole Kernel Corn, 425g
  • 50 g cheese powder
  • 100 g margarine or butter



Procedure

  1. Drain Jolly Whole Corn and place in a bowl.

  2. Add some cheese powder and melted margarine or butter.

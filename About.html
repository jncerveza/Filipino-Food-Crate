Filipino foods have been from the origins of the Austronesian (along with Malaysian and Indonesian cuisine) to centuries of mixed cuisine influenced by China, Spain, and the United States, along with a large wave of influence that enriched the culture of the archipelago. And over the years, those influenced foods evolved and Filipinos love and adopt those influenced as their own.

This website is a compilation of some different foods here in the Philippines. The purpose of this website is to let those viewers be informed about Filipino Foods, Give ideas about what Filipino foods look like, and know the recipes of each food on this website. This website is consists of the dishes, desserts, and snacks that the Philippines have. And the objective of this portfolio is to share knowledge about Filipino food and how to make them. And to promote Filipino culture deeper in our country and also spread it to other countries.

And this website also presents the effort of different person recipes and appreciates their effort in taking pictures, sharing recipes, and posting them on their website.

Mango Graham

History

Mango Graham or Mango Float is a Filipino icebox cake with layers of graham crackers and between those layers is spreaded whipped cream or condensed milk and carabao mangoes . Mango Float is a modern variant of the traditional Filipino crema de fruta cake. There is also a different type of this dessert, its called Crema de mangga. This dessert uses Crema de mangga custard and gulaman (agar) or gelatin, as in the original crema de fruta. Mango Graham is usually stored inside a refrigerator before being served, so that the layers will not slip off while eating it also to make the whipped cream slightly solid.

Ingredients

  • 24 pieces Graham crackers (20-24 pieces)
  • 500 ml all-purpose cream (2 packs)
  • 300 ml sweetened condensed milk (1 can)
  • 4 small-medium ripe mangoes
  • White Sugar
  • Keso (Optional)

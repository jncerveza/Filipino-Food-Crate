Binatog

Binatog a popular Filipino street food and snack that gives a different kind of taste and senstaion. It is made by soaking the mature white corn in water and salt until puffed, the soaked corns are then boiled until the skin is peeled off. It is topped with boiled corn kernels, grated coconut, salt and sugar.

Pancit Canton

Pancit canton is a famous and a favorite snack of the Filipinos. It is a stir-fried noodle here in the Philippines made from wheat-flour egg noodles, and vegetables.

Filipino Style Spaghetti

Filipino-style spaghetti is a snack and served on any special occasion. It has a sweet sauce which is the favorite of the Filipinos and makes it unique.

Pancit Molo

Pancit Molo is a type of soup using wonton wrappers which is originated from Molo district in Iloilo City. It is made by a mixture of ground pork wrapped in molo or wonton wrapper, shredded chicken meat, and also shrimps. The soup is ladled into serving bowls, garnished with green onions and fried garlic bit for another layer of flavor.

Palabok

Palabok is a traditional Filipino dish which contains thin rice noodles doused in a creamy shrimp-infused sauce. It is topped with various toppings such as hard-boiled eggs, pork cracklings, shrimps, pork, fish flakes, and scallions.

Inihaw na isaw

Isaw is one of the street food ihaw-ihaw and a favorite snack of the Filipinos. Ihaw-ihaw is grilled street food and on a skewer..

Cheesy Corn

Cheesy corn is street food and a favorite snack of everyone. It consists of kernel corn, powder cheese, and butter only.

Ginataang Bilo Bilo

Ginataang Bilo Bilo is one of the famous and favorite snacks or desserts of the Filipinos because of its sweetness and creaminess. Ginataang Bilo Bilo is cooked in sweet coconut milk together with mixed fruits

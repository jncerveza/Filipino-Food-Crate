Chicken Igado

Igado is a dish that uses pork tenderloin and pig innards, which are popular in the Ilocos region. Cooked with a mixture of vinegar and soy sauce, it brings out a unique flavor. It has vegetables such as peas and red peppers that give the dish a beautiful and vibrant color.

Chicken Pastel

Chicken Pastel is a hearty stew of diced chicken, sausages, mushrooms, peas and carrots stewed in a rich creamy sauce. Based on Spanish pastel de pollo, the chicken filling is wrapped in flaky puff pastry and baked until golden.

Chop Suey

Chop Suey's recipe features a multitude of vegetables and meats. It's easy to saute vegetables in a thick sauce, ideal for special occasions or for a healthy everyday meal.

Crispy Pata

Crispy Pata is a snack for pork lovers. It has crispy pork skin filled with spicy and tender meat. Crispy Pata is commonly defined as fried pork ribs or shanks, but are actually cut from the hock to the feet.

Dinuguan

Dinuguan, also known as chocolate meat or blood pudding, is a hearty dish made from cubic pork, pork blood and spices. This classic Filipino pork stew is generous and tasty, and is delicious as a main course of rice or as a mid day snack with puto.

Escabeche

Escabeche is usually fish or meat, marinated and/or cooked in vinegar and a little spice. However, in the Philippines, the fish is first fried and then dipped in sweet and sour sauce, or marinated sweet and sour pork.

Ginataang Kalabasa

Ginataang Kalabasa is a vegetable stew that is popular in the Filipino culture. It is made with these two main ingredients, Kalabasa also known as Kabocha squash and coconut milk. It usually includes shrimp and yardlong beans and bagoong or patis. It can also be cooked with fish, crab or meat and a variety of other ingredients.

Giniling

Pork Giniling is a dish that uses ground pork as its main ingredient. It's also one of the easiest recipes to put together. All you need is minced meat and some vegetables (potatoes, carrots, green peas).

Ginisang Ampalaya

Ginisang Ampalaya is a delicious vegetable stir-fry made with bitter melon, tomatoes, and eggs. It's delicious, nutritious and budget friendly.

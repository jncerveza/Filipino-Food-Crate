Pancit Canton

Pancit canton is a famous and a favorite snack of the Filipinos. It is a stir-fried noodle here in the Philippines made from wheat-flour egg noodles, and vegetables.

Mamon

Mamon is a soft, buttery, and fluffy chiffon or sponge cake. Best snacks for kids or even among adults.

Otap

Otap is a food common in Cebu where it is certainly originated. It is an oval-shapped puff pastry cookie. The pastry must undergo 11 baking stages to achieve the texture.

Piaya

Piaya is a food common in Negros Occidental where it is a popular food because of its deliciousness. It is a muscuvado-filled unleavened flatbread. It is made by filling dough with a mixture of muscovado and glucose syrup.

Banana Cue

Banana cue is a popular street food in the Philippines and it gives banana flavoured sweetness. It is made with saba bananas coated with sugar. To make the sugar coat the banana it is deep fried and skewered on a stick.

Maruya

Maruya is a famous fanned shape street food in the streets of the Philippines. It is made from saba bananas and egg mixed flour. For its masterpiece appearance it is then deep fried and sprinkled with sugar.

Empanada

Empanada is famous around the world, especially in Latin America, Spain, and Portugal. They are made by folded dough over the filling and sealing it before cooking.

Sagot Gulaman is a popular drink among Filipinos by its chilling sensation. It is made from a bar of or powdered form of dried agar or carrageenan used to make jelly-like desserts. It is served as iced or cooled.

Pancit Molo

Pancit Molo is a type of soup using wonton wrappers which is originated from Molo district in Iloilo City. It is made by a mixture of ground pork wrapped in molo or wonton wrapper, shredded chicken meat, and also shrimps. The soup is ladled into serving bowls, garnished with green onions and fried garlic bit for another layer of flavor.

Palabok

Palabok is a traditional Filipino dish which contains thin rice noodles doused in a creamy shrimp-infused sauce. It is topped with various toppings such as hard-boiled eggs, pork cracklings, shrimps, pork, fish flakes, and scallions.

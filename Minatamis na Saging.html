Minatamis na Saging

≪




Source:https://www.lutongpinoyrecipe.com/desserts/minatamis-na-saging/








History

Minatamis na Saging or Sweetened Banana in English, is made with chopped saba bananas[2] cooked in a sweet syrup (arniba). But you can also add different ingredients to be added like sweet potato, sago, or other fruits like jackfruit.Minatamis na saging could also be added to a shaved iced with a syrup, now this is called saba kon-yelo.

Make One

Ingredients

By LUTONG PINOY RECIPE.com
  • 10 pcs of saba (you can sliced it or its up to you)
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 cups water

Procedure

  • In a saucepan, combine brown sugar and water. Let it boil.
  • As soon as it boils, add the saba and cook for 15-20 minutes or until tender.
  • Let it cool down and serve.
